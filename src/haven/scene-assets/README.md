# Safe Haven scene art

Drop illustration files here and they become the scene backdrops (with animated
overlays — fire flicker, rain, dust, parallax — layered on top automatically).

## Naming

- `cabin-0.jpg`, `cabin-1.jpg`, `cabin-2.jpg` — the three cabin camera angles (spots 1/2/3)
- `beach-0.jpg`, `beach-1.jpg`, `beach-2.jpg` — the three beach angles
- `city-0.jpg`, `city-1.jpg`, `city-2.jpg` — the three highrise angles

Shortcut: a single `cabin.jpg` (no number) is used for all three cabin spots.
Same for `beach.jpg` / `city.jpg`. Start with one per theme, add angles later.

`.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp` all work. Any missing file falls back to the
built-in procedural scene, so partial sets are fine.

## What each angle should show (matches the inspiration)

- **cabin-0**: fireplace centred, two seats in the foreground, bookshelves either side
- **cabin-1**: from the right chair — fireplace on the right, window + city on the left
- **cabin-2**: window + chair on the left, fireplace in the background on the right
- **beach-0**: sun/moon centred on the horizon, a bench on the pier looking out
- **beach-1**: hut on the right, palms + sun on the left
- **beach-2**: hut on the left, palms + sun on the right
- **city-0**: skyline through the window, bed with book + mug
- **city-1**: desk by the window (laptop, lamp, plant)
- **city-2**: sofa + coffee table, floor-to-ceiling sunset windows

Landscape (~16:10) images look best — the haven opens in landscape on phone.
Keep files reasonably sized (≈1600px wide, < 1 MB each) for fast loading.
